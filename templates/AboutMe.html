About Me

Hello! My Name is Chris!

I've been a writer for the last six years. During that time, I wrote essays, Blog posts, SEO articles, and copy. During my last two years, I began using my knowledge as an editor to take freelance editing jobs.
Before this, I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a Bachelor's degree in Philosophy. After I left school, I spent the next two years working as a dog trainer.
Animal training and Operant Conditioning would eventually introduce me to my current love: data analytics. I would later go back to UCSD and receive a certificate in Data Analytics and Visualization.
After completing a few projects, I'm hoping to find more work to help me challenge and grow my love for analytics.
