My name is Chris! Welcome to my Data portfolio.

Below you will find samples of my data related projects.

PuppyZon

Have you ever seen a dog online and thought "I would love to adopt a dog just like that!" My team and I created PuppyZon to help! Puppyzon uses a neural network to identify the type of dog in an uploaded photo, and then find you a similar dog on Petfinder.

Youtube Stats

Who is making the most money on Youtube? Where are they from? Based on data from the top 100 youtube channels, we can provide insight into what a top youtube channel looks like.

Data Jobs: An Analysis

What do most data jobs want out of candidates? What's the most useful skill to learn if you want to get a job in data science? We scraped several popular job-posting websites and created visualizations for you to enjoy!
